# About Dr. Ryan Stewart

<img src="/assets/images/ryan-stewart.jpg" alt="Dr. Ryan Stewart" style="float: right; margin: 0 0 1rem 1rem; width: 200px; border-radius: 8px; box-shadow: 0 2px 5px rgba(0,0,0,0.15);" />

I'm a board-certified, fellowship-trained urogynecologist practicing in Green Bay, Wisconsin. My practice focuses entirely on female pelvic floor disorders — the conditions that don't always get talked about openly, but that affect daily life in real ways.

## Credentials and training

- **Board certified** in Obstetrics and Gynecology and in Urogynecology and Reconstructive Pelvic Surgery (American Board of Obstetrics and Gynecology)
- **Fellowship-trained** in Female Pelvic Medicine and Reconstructive Surgery (urogynecology)
- Trained at Via College of Osteopathic Medicine, Indiana University School of Medicine, and the University of Louisville School of Medicine

## What I treat

I specialize in the diagnosis and treatment of:

- [Pelvic organ prolapse](/conditions/pelvic-organ-prolapse)
- [Urinary incontinence](/conditions/urinary-incontinence)
- Overactive bladder
- Fecal incontinence
- Other pelvic floor disorders

You can see the full list on my [conditions](/conditions) and [treatments](/treatments) pages.

## My approach

Every person's situation is different, and so is the right plan. I believe in shared decision making: I'll walk you through the options — from conservative measures to surgery — explain the trade-offs honestly, and help you choose the path that fits your goals and your life. There's no single "correct" order to try things in; the best choice is the one we make together.

## Research and publications

Beyond clinical care, I'm active in research on surgical outcomes for pelvic organ prolapse and stress urinary incontinence, patient education, and postoperative recovery. You can browse my [peer-reviewed publications](/research).
